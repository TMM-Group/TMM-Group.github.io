Dr. Gang Tang

Associate Professor, Beijing Institute of Technology

Website | Scholar | GitHub

Gang Tang is an Associate Professor at the Advanced Research Institute of Multidisciplinary Science, Beijing Institute of Technology. His research focuses on using materials simulation, machine learning, and data science to design new materials for tackling critical challenges, including photovoltaics, ferroelectrics, piezoelectrics, and thermoelectrics.

Gang Tang received his PhD from Beijing Institute of Technology in 2019.
